This page features a visualization of the impact of public research institutes (PRI) on the Polish economy, based on their patenting activity. Through detailed analysis, we are able to provide a comprehensive look at how these institutes are contributing to various economic sectors in Poland. Whether you are interested in the analysis of patenting activity by usage in NACE Rev 2 sectors our visualization has you covered.
